My projects

Creating music is my challenge...

I love to improvise creating beats, design songs of all styles, think in chords and melodies and experiment with samples. I use audio libraries and midi controllers to design the sound of any instrument. My favorite styles are rock, pop, blues and lofi. But I am flexible, I am always waiting for new sounds.

I like working on online projects, mixing and mastering is one of my great passions. I have my collection of plugins like Waves, Izotope, Softube, SSL, FabFilter. My favorite DAWs are Pro Tools, Cakewalk and Ableton live.

I also like to transcribe music, especially related to string instruments like guitar, bass and ukulele. It is a great challenge to transcribe and adapt songs to these instruments. For this kind of work I use Guitar Pro.
